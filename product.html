About us

Calculations can be one of the main sources of fatigue and burnout when creating a certain behavior in a game. Unex or more commonly known as the Drag and Drop Game Engine caters to the aspiring game developers who wish to create their vision on their game without any prior experience. This tool helps remove the difficulty of coding and game designing to fit the user's needs.

Are you tired of typing, reading, and debugging tons of code?

Are you worried about how long would it take to finish a certain task?

Do you want to be productive and achieve more doing less?




If you say yes to any of these questions, then this is for you!

Mission

Is to help improve the game industry breed young talents into the platform. This not only improves the overall image of the high execution barrier the gaming industry have. But also helps the developer realize the game of their dreams.


Vision

To emulate the feeling of drawing on a canvass. The overall freedom of expression a piece of art has is what we aim to translate onto the game engine.
